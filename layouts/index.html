About Me

Hey! I’m an aerospace engineering student who likes to code and make stuff!

Bio

As long as I can remember I’m deeply passionate about aviation and space. Engineering and coding crossed my way since the high-school and become a field discovery and creation. I believe that this had a strong influence from the Star Wars saga, which I’m a huge fan of.

I’m from São Paulo, Brazil, living in Belo Horizonte (or Beagá).

I occasionally blog here, also there, and there. I’m also a hobbyist musician at Reversão de Comando, a cool band named after an aircraft effect that happens at high speeds.

Interests

Currently, I’m focused in aeroelasticity, using, and developing software to analyze aeroelastic phenomena.

Although, I’ve interests in the electrification of vehicles, distributed propulsion, hypersonic flight, morphing wings, aircraft conceptual design, and multidisciplinary optimization.

I’m also interested in gliders, music (mostly string instruments and sound engineering), and fiction movies and books.

Experience
